## Overall Evaluation

### 1. Working Environment

The internship environment was friendly and professional. Everyone was willing to provide support whenever I encountered difficulties and created opportunities for me to learn throughout the internship. The comfortable working environment helped me stay focused and complete my tasks effectively.

### 2. Support from Mentor and Team

My mentor provided clear guidance, shared practical experience, and helped me overcome challenges during the internship. The team was also supportive and encouraged me to take initiative in learning and solving problems independently.

### 3. Relevance to My Major

The assigned tasks were closely related to my academic background and allowed me to explore various AWS technologies. This internship gave me the opportunity to apply theoretical knowledge to a real-world project while improving my technical skills.

### 4. Learning Opportunities

During the internship, I had the chance to learn about various AWS services, participate in workshops and technical events, and contribute to a real project. These experiences helped me better understand cloud technologies and professional workflows.

### 5. Working Culture

The company promotes a collaborative and respectful working environment. I always felt encouraged to share my ideas and work together with my teammates to achieve common goals.

### 6. Internship Program

The internship program was well organized with a clear learning roadmap. In addition to technical tasks, interns had opportunities to join knowledge-sharing sessions and industry events, which greatly enhanced the learning experience.

## Additional Questions

- **What satisfied you the most during the internship?**  
  Having the opportunity to work on a real project, learn from my mentor, and gain hands-on experience with AWS services.

- **What do you think could be improved?**  
  Organizing more knowledge-sharing sessions and networking activities among interns.

- **Would you recommend this internship program to others? Why?**  
  Yes. It provides a great environment to gain practical experience, improve technical skills, and build a solid foundation for a career in Cloud Computing.

## Suggestions

- Organize more workshops and seminars on AWS and AI technologies.
- Provide interns with more opportunities to participate in real-world projects.
- Continue strengthening the interaction between mentors and interns to support learning and professional development.
